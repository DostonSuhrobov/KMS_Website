Warehouse Services

Roadtex services a customer base with requirements of warehouse storage, order selection, container unloading, cross-dock, pick and packing, repacking, shrink packaging, over wrapping, conversion projects UCC 128, UPC and standard label generation and more. We are one stop shopping and can fulfill all of your business needs. All you need to do is manufacture, sell it and leave the rest to us!

Warehouse Management System

Our WMS (warehouse management system) is web – based and manages orders, inventory, EDI and allows our customers to view reports. Our system manages code and expiration dates, maintains receipts, tracks shipments, and accepts manual and EDI orders. Capability to produce and send all EDI transactions required by Big Box consignees is managed through our WMS with consistent updates to remain compliant. Every inbound pallet receives it's unique pallet ID which tracks the “life “of the pallet via our locator system through shipment. All outbound orders have system generated pallet placards and as needed UCC128 labels are printed on site at pallet or case level. This system services the most sophisticated and allows the very basic to grow.

Facilities & Warehouse Services

Our facilities accommodate and comply with food grade, and confectionery products. Roadtex prides itself on facility organization and cleanliness. The facilities are temperature controlled and are monitored daily for high and low temperature levels. Also included in our facilities layouts are specified zones for staging inbounds, outbounds, repacking and cross dock distribution business types. The facilities are sized to handle any customer and manage any storage, shipping, cross dock distribution and repacking needs.

Our focus is to continue servicing clients and deliver the full service model for temperature controlled product requirements. We understand our customers manufacture and sell, and Roadtex can manage the rest with the highest level of professionalism. Whether your business is small, medium or large, our focus is to deliver superior service. We are committed to produce the highest quality for the end user and allow our customers to maintain longevity by fairly pricing our service.

Roadtex has multiple facilities to accommodate the confectionery and food industry. All of our warehouse facilities are temperature controlled and monitored in real time. We currently manage accounts ranging from 20,000 pallets in dedicated facilities to small cross dock accounts. Based on the account, our services range from, pick/pack, pool distribution, drayage, container unloading, to extensive customer display building.
