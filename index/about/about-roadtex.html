About Roadtex

Since 1992, Roadtex has become a leader in the nationwide LTL industry. We are able to handle all of your refrigerated and LTL needs by leveraging:

  • Our nationwide network of 31 temperature controlled facilities
  • State of the art tracking
  • Specialized equipment and warehousing capabilities
  • No sailing schedules

Roadtex Transportation's Corporate Headquarters are based in Somerset, New Jersey. The company was founded in 1992 by Bob Kelly and Bruno Ciacciarelli and has grown to be an asset based company with 31 strategically located facilities. Based on the principles of safety and respect, the company has grown from it's inception to one that has invested in its own equipment, drivers, and terminals to better serve our valued customers. We have become a leader in the nationwide LTL industry, specializing in the transportation of time and temperature sensitive products. We provide services that our customers need to remain competitive and profitable in their industry. Roadtex Transportation – a company with the knowledge, vision and driving force making all of your shipments a successful delivery.

Mission Statement

Achieving continuous growth by providing valuable services that enable our partners to be successful in their business with their customers. Roadtex through consistent development of customer service, communications and advanced technologies builds a seamless partnership between customer and vendor.
